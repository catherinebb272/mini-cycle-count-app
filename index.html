🔖 Barcode Sheets for Faster Write-Offs

Instead of manually typing SKUs during write-offs, generate printable barcode sheets. The person doing write-offs can scan a barcode directly into DRS — fast and accurate.

  1. 1. After approving write-offs, download the SKU list
  2. 2. Upload that list to a barcode generator (100x300 size)
  3. 3. Upload the generated barcodes back here
  4. 4. Download formatted sheets — 5 across × 22 down (110/page)

Print settings: Scale set to "Shrink to Fit"

⚠️ Important

  • • Not a substitute for full physical inventory — This tool helps you catch drift and manage shrinkage between counts, but it won't replace a proper RGIS or Datascan full count.
  • • Use results cautiously — Always verify high-value items before writing off. When in doubt, skip it and investigate manually.
  • • Best for targeted counts — Ideal for monthly category checks, pre-clearance verification, or tracking specific bins.

🔖 Create Barcode Sheets

Generate printable barcode sheets for your write-off items. This lets you scan barcodes directly into DRS instead of typing SKUs manually.

Step 1: Get Barcodes

  1. Download the write-off CSV from the previous step
  2. Go to a barcode generator (like Barcode.tec-it.com)
  3. Select Code 128 format, size 100×300
  4. Upload your CSV to generate barcodes, then download the PDF
